Roti/Flatbread

A simple Yet Delicious Flatbread!

Roti is the daily bread for millions of Indians.

A great addition to every meal

This is one of the tastiest and simple bread outhere.

+++Ingredients+++

  • Wheat Flour 1 Cup
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tbsp. Oil
  • Water depends on dough
  • Rolling pin
  • Ghee for Garnish

+++Instructions+++

  1. To make roti, combine the flour, Oil and salt in bowl and knead into a soft ball. Keep aside for 20 Mins.
  2. Divide the dough into 8 equal portions and roll out each portion into a circle.
  3. heat a non stick griddle on a high flame and place roti gently over it.
  4. Cook it till its full of air and starts to bloat.
  5. Smear Ghee on it and serve Hot
